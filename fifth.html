MIA AND THE UNDEAD

CHAPTER 1:DID YOU SAY SOMETHING, MIJA?

I screamed. I said that I would scream. I warned him before that I would.

"Mija, what is going on in here?" comes from the door before it is flung wide open.

"Daaad," I whined, hoping it would make him go away faster.

"What happened, Mia?" he pressed. "Are you alright?" "Did you hurt yourself?" "Are you hurt?"

"Dad," I said again, "I'm fiiine.Seriously. I'm okay." He did not look convinced.

"I'm not convinced," he said.

Ayayay. What am I gonna do with you, dad?

"Well," I started,"Why not?"

He squinted his eyes at me, then slapped his hand over his face, shaking his head. He said, "You shriek like the devil, mija. Half of the neighbors must be awake. I know that their dogs are."

"Yeah, right," I told him. I walked away from my closet and over to my bed, diving unto it. "Those dogs never sleep. They might as well be zombies. I'm sure they'd eat my brain if I let them."

He sighed. "Not the point here, Mia. Why did you scream?"

I rolled my eyes back.I braced myself up against the wooden headboard, and pulled my sneakers off. After tossing them off of the bed, I pointed up to a top corner of my room.

"Are you serious, Mija?"

I grinned sheepishly at him. "Get rid of it, please?"

He left the room and came back with a trash-bin and a broom, and went to work.

"The spider's gone now. Try to get some sleep," he said to me as he made his way to the door. "Good Night, mija. I love you."

"I love you too, dad. Can you get my light before you leave?"

"Sure thing," he said."No more screaming, okay?"

"I make no promises," I told him.

I watched as he shook his head and switched the light off. I heard the door get shut behind him.

The room stayed deadly quiet. I spoke out loud, "Told you I was gonna scream."

"Haha, hilarious," responds the voice from the closet.

"Next time you leave your body parts lying around on my bed I will beat you back to life with them, understood?"

"If you say so, dear," says my Undead Boyfriend.
